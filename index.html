About Us

Founded in 2005 by Eduardo "Ka ED" Santos, our store has become a beloved destination for authentic Filipino barbecue. What began as a small roadside stall has grown into a thriving business thanks to our loyal customers and commitment to quality.

We use traditional family recipes passed down through generations, combined with the freshest local ingredients to create unforgettable dining experiences.

  • 100% authentic Filipino recipes
  • Locally sourced ingredients
  • Family-owned and operated

Our Mission

To bring people together through quality barbecue experiences while promoting sustainable practices and honoring our Filipino culinary roots.

We are committed to:

  • Using only the freshest, locally-sourced ingredients
  • Preserving traditional Filipino cooking methods
  • Providing exceptional service to every customer
  • Supporting our local community

Our Vision

To become a nationwide BBQ brand known for authentic flavors, outstanding service, and community impact.

We envision a future where:

  • Filipino barbecue is celebrated worldwide
  • Our store becomes a destination for food lovers
  • We can share our culinary heritage with more people
  • We create opportunities for our employees and community

Ordering Help

How do I place an order?

You can place an order through our website or mobile app by selecting your items, choosing pickup or delivery, and completing the checkout process.

Can I modify my order after placing it?

Orders can be modified within 5 minutes of placement. Please call our store directly at (02) 8123 4567 for immediate assistance.

What are your operating hours?

We're open daily from 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM, including holidays. Delivery hours are from 11:00 AM to 9:30 PM.

Delivery Information

What areas do you deliver to?

We currently deliver within a 5km radius of our store location. Enter your address during checkout to confirm if we deliver to your area.

How long does delivery take?

Average delivery time is 30-45 minutes depending on your location and current order volume.
